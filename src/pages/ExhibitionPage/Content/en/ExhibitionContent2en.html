We invite you to a unique exhibition, one of the world's top art events, where the art of the legendary Maria Prymachenko will reveal to you an unexplored dimension of Ukrainian folk modernism. Her bright, whimsical creations and fantastic compositions captivated Pablo Picasso and Salvador Dali, and her works are included in the golden fund of world art.

What awaits you?

  • Original works by Prymachenko, combining Ukrainian folklore, surrealism and deep philosophy.
  • Interactive zones where you can delve into the process of creating her unique paintings.
  • A multimedia exhibition that will bring fantastic beasts and floral compositions to life, revealing the artist's cosmic vision.

Why is it worth seeing?

  • World-renowned: Maria Prymachenko's works have been exhibited at the Louvre, New York's MoMA, and the Tate Modern in London.
  • The cultural code of Ukraine: its paintings are a cipher of the national soul, where the past and the future are intertwined in magical symbols.
  • An emotional explosion: immerse yourself in a world where amazing creatures seem to talk to you, and colors vibrate like music.

Don't miss the chance to see a living legend of world art, who once again proves: Ukraine is the cradle of geniuses who change the world!
