We invite you to a unique exhibition where you can not only see, but also create your own masterpiece, inspired by the richness of Ukrainian culture and modern trends..

What awaits you?

  • Master classes from leading Ukrainian artists, where you can master techniques of embroidery, pottery and other folk crafts.
  • Interactive zones, where each visitor can try themselves as an artist or sculptor.
  • A contemporary art exhibition that demonstrates how traditional motifs are integrated into modern design and fashion.

Why visit?

  • Cultural immersion: learn more about Ukrainian traditions and their influence on contemporary art.
  • Hands-on experience: the opportunity to create something unique and take your work home.
  • Inspiration: discover new facets of creativity and perhaps find a new hobby.

Come and create your masterpiece, inspired by the richness of Ukrainian culture and contemporary art!
